SchedulerAI

AI-Powered Meeting Scheduler  ·  Product Overview for Management

✦ Production Ready

SchedulerAI lets you schedule meetings using plain English. It connects with Google Calendar and Zoom, extracts meeting details with AI, detects conflicts, and sends invites — all in under 30 seconds.

Current Features
🤖 AI Extraction 📅 Google Calendar 🎥 Zoom Integration 🔐 Google OAuth ⚡ Smart Slot Finder 🚨 Priority Detection 📋 Agenda View 📧 Auto Invites 🔄 Multi-AI Providers
🤖 Natural Language Scheduling
Type raw meeting notes or plain sentences like "Schedule a budget review with John tomorrow at 3 PM for 1 hour". AI extracts title, participants, date, time, and duration automatically.
📅 Real-Time Calendar Availability
Connects to Google Calendar via OAuth to check busy slots in real time. If your chosen time is taken, it suggests the nearest available alternatives automatically.
🎥 Google Meet & Zoom
Supports two meeting platforms. Google Meet links are generated automatically. Zoom uses Server-to-Server OAuth for enterprise-grade integration — user picks the platform before booking.
📧 Automatic Invite Dispatch
Once a slot is confirmed, calendar invites are sent to all participants instantly via Google Calendar. No manual copy-paste of links or emails needed.
⚡ Smart Missing Field Recovery
If any required detail is missing (e.g. no time or email), the system asks one targeted follow-up question at a time rather than showing a big form. Keeps the UX conversational.
🚨 Priority & Urgency Detection
Detects keywords like "urgent", "critical", "ASAP" from meeting notes and assigns a priority badge (High / Medium / Low) to each scheduled meeting automatically.
